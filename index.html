A new generation of DayZ server.

We want our work to exist as something of a love letter to DayZ as a platform. All the glory, all the despair. A unique, balanced, thoughtful and immersive experience - a new generation of DayZ server connecting multiple maps to one overall hub.

Balanced Combat.

Weapons, armour, and melee have been thoughtfully balanced, to provide a combat system that feels deep, reactive, and realistic.

Your survival will depend on your adapatability. You must ration your bullets carefully, and plan for each encounter with keen situational awareness.

Full details can be found on our discord, and will also be available shortly on our wiki.

Rewarding Loot.

Loot tables undergo frequent rebalancing, ensuring that no items are too easy or too difficult to obtain.

Weapons are plentiful, and supplies can be ordered remotely if needed, but end-game gear will be an end-game challenge to obtain.

We wanted to eliminate the familiar frustration of playing DayZ only to starve, die of a random sickness, or quit due to lack of loot. You'll have no issues with your first few hours of survival, but from there you're on your own.

Environmental Threats.

Zombies are variable in strength depending on their location, and can deal increased damage, wear armour, climb and open doors, and take reduced damage.

This all depends on the location you are in, with more volatile locations exposed to higher levels during the outbreak housing tougher zombies.

End-game areas such as gas and radiation zones are deadly without adequate preparation, so ensure you are ready before proceeding.

Seamless Progression.

Your character persists across all [PsyOps] servers, so your gear, weapons, and vehicles, can be accessed whichever server you'd like to use them on.

Track your progress with metrics using our highscores powered by CFTools, unlock achievements, and uncover the secrets of our post-apocalyptic militarised worlds.

Craft, gather, and mine your way to victory with our peer-to-peer economy, which enables the sale of resources, weapons, crafting components, and more!
